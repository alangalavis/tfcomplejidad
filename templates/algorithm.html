What is the program about?

The proposal consists on simulating a movie recommendation platform, in which users can filter movies based on parameters.

How does the algorithm work?

The information provided by the dataset is stored in an undirected graph data structure, where nodes are represented by movie IDs, and edges correspond to the relationships between movies. The edges' weights are determined by user-selected parameters (Such as genre, studio and overview), an idea that emerged from researching about content-based filtering. This technique is employed to classify content as relevant or not relevant to users, providing personalized recommendations.
